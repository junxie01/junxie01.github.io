## Hello!

Welcome to **SEISAMUSE** — my personal corner of the internet where I write about seismology, geophysics, and the occasional tangent into environmental and glacial seismology.

### Why This Site?

As a researcher, I believe in open knowledge sharing. This site serves as:

- A **blog** for my thoughts on recent papers, field experiences, and research insights.
- A **portfolio** showcasing my projects and resources.
- A way to connect with fellow researchers and the broader geoscience community.

### What to Expect

I'll be posting about:

1. **Paper reviews** — My take on interesting new publications.
2. **Research notes** — Methods, tools, and tips from the trenches.
3. **Field stories** — Adventures (and misadventures) in data collection.
4. **Tutorials** — Practical guides for seismology and data analysis.

### Stay Connected

Feel free to explore the [Blog](/blog/), check out my [Projects & Resources](/links/), or learn more [About Me](/about/).

If you find this site useful, consider supporting my work — every bit of encouragement helps!
